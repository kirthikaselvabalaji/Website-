Services that we provide

BBC Construction Company offers a wide range of services, including:
Interior and Exterior Construction:BBC specializes in both interior and exterior construction work, ensuring high-quality finishes for residential and commercial properties.
Panchayat Drawings: They provide architectural drawings and plans that comply with the guidelines and regulations set by local panchayats, ensuring smooth approvals for rural projects.
Municipality Drawings: BBC Company prepares detailed drawings and documentation that adhere to municipal regulations, facilitating approvals for urban construction projects.
DTCP Approval Drawings: For projects falling under the jurisdiction of the Directorate of Town and Country Planning (DTCP), BBC Company prepares drawings and documentation to secure the necessary approvals.
BBC Construction Company is your reliable partner for all your construction needs, ensuring compliance with local regulations and delivering quality construction services.

Interior Design

Transform your interiors into stunning and inviting spaces with our interior design services. Our skilled designers will work with you to choose the right color schemes, furniture, lighting, and decor elements to create a cohesive and visually appealing environment that reflects your personality and taste.

Commercial Building

If you're planning to construct or renovate a commercial building, look no further. Our expertise in commercial construction spans various sectors, including office spaces, retail establishments, warehouses, and more. We understand the unique requirements of commercial projects and are dedicated to delivering functional and visually appealing spaces that meet your business needs.

Facade designing

Enhance the aesthetic appeal and functionality of your building's facade with our facade designing expertise. We focus on creating innovative and sustainable facade solutions that not only improve the building's appearance but also contribute to energy efficiency and environmental sustainability.

Residential Building

From custom home builds to remodeling, we specialize in creating beautiful and functional residential spaces. Our team works closely with you to design and construct your dream home, ensuring it meets your unique needs and style preferences

Presentation drawings

Our presentation drawing services are designed to help you visualize your project conceptually. We create detailed and visually appealing drawings and renderings that provide a clear representation of the design, making it easier for you to make informed decisions and present your ideas to stakeholders.

DTCP Approval

We assist you in obtaining the necessary approvals from the relevant authorities, such as the Directorate of Town and Country Planning (DTCP). Our team prepares meticulous DTCP approval drawings and documentation, ensuring that your project complies with all regulatory requirements and standards.
